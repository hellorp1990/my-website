Rahul Paul

 

 

 

 

Bio:  I have received the M.Tech. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad, India. I am currently a fifth year CSE Ph.D. candidate at the University of South Florida, Tampa, USA. I am part of the Computer Vision research group of USF and Radiomics Group at MOFFITT CANCER CENTER with Dr. Robert Gillies and Dr. Matthew Schabath . I am advised by Professor: Dmitry Goldgof and Professor: Lawrence Hall.

Curriculum Vitae

 

Research interest: I have a broader interest in artificial intelligence. My current research focuses on Medical Image Processing, Image Processing, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Data Mining in the context of Radiomics and Neonatal Pain Analysis.
